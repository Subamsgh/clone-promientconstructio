Residential Roofing in Minnesota is unlike many parts of the country since there are so many different types of roofs that are offered: asphalt roofs, cedar shake roofs, tile roofs, metal roofs, and flat roofs. Because of our experience doing so many residential and commercial roofing projects over the years in the Twin Cities area, Prominent has an advantage over many other local companies.

We understand that any roofing projects can be quite an investment, especially when roofs here are prone to harsh weather and damage from age, the sun, and our extremely cold winters. That is why we have the experience and training to deliver excellence with our work. We offer everything that yours needs to stay maintained and to keep your house in good shape for years to come.
